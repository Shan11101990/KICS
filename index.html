KICS PROJECTS

CTSR A Division of KICS

CTSR focused on enhancing practical abilities, technical knowledge, and competencies to improve employability and bridge the gap between education and industry requirements. The center offers skill development training, certifications, and hands-on woshops in various disciplines, such as IT, engineering, digital, advanced technology and specialized trades.

KICS MARKETING A Division of KICS

A dedicated team of professional enthusiasts on online and e-commerce business. Buying and selling of goods, services, or data over the internet, utilizing digital platforms like websites, apps, or social media to facilitate transactions. It supports KICS Eco System to promote the members products and services.

JANSEVAK A Division of KICS

An innovative project aimed at bridging start up, entrepreneurship, B2B, B2C, business consultation and online services to the public under a single roof.

KICS ENERGY SOLAR A Division of KICS

It involves designing, manufacturing, installing, and maintaining solar power systems, including PV panels, to convert sunlight into electricity for commercial or residential use with subsidies and benefits.

KICS SCHEMES & PROGRAMS

NAVABHARATHI

Women Development Program The scheme offers assistance to women entrepreneurs to establish or enhance their businesses, promoting women’s economic independence and promote self-employment.

NAIPUNYA BHARATH

Skill Development & Training Program It offers industry-relevant training and placement assistance to students and professionals. It focuses on industrial and technology training to enhance employability.

YUVABHARATH

Youth Development Program The program aim to build a skilled, self-reliant workforce and foster civic engagement among new generation. The initiative offers assistance to enhance entrepreneurship and self-employment.

JEEVABHARATH

CSR Initiatives The program is uniquely designed for social development of communities. We promote sustainability, Employee giving programs, Volunteer opportunities, Carbon footprint reduction Etc.

KICS PARIVAR
