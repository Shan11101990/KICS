Mission

To mold a sustainable society that can contribute to the country’s progress, social, cultural, economic and educational development.

Vision

Formulate and implement sustainable action plans. Empower the community by providing jobs and skills. Adopt and develop innovative technologies necessary for the advancement and development of the team. Ensure quality and efficiency of services. Emphasis on progress and development of the country.

Our Values

Unity | Reciprocity | Integrity | Innovation

The members are the true strength of the group and their welfare, progress and happiness are the priority of the group. The basic principle is honesty, mutual trust, love and co-existence among the members. Act ethically and transparently, Innovative and progressive ideas should be given importance.
